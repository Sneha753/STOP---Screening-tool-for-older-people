Instructions for the Colorvision Test

Welcome to the Colorvision test. Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the test:

  • The Color Vision Test is designed to assess your ability to distinguish different colors and numbers hidden within colorful patterns.
  • Enter the number you see in the text box below each image.
  • Carefully observe each image and ensure you input the correct number in the respective text box.
  • An accuracy percentage will also be shown, reflecting your performance.
  • Click on the 'Start Test' button when you are ready to begin.

Instructions for the Vision Test

Welcome to the Vision test. Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the test:

  • The Tumbling E Test is designed to assess your visual acuity by identifying the smallest letter you can see clearly.
  • For each set of images, choose the smallest letter that you can see clearly by clicking on it.
  • Answer all questions to the best of your ability.
  • An accuracy percentage will also be shown, reflecting your performance.
  • Click on the 'Start Test' button when you are ready to begin.

Instructions for the DateTime Test

Welcome to the DateTime test. Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the test:

  • The required format for entering the date and time is YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM.
  • Complete the test promptly.
  • Example: For January 2, 2000, at 2:00 PM, you should enter 2000-01-02 14:00.
  • Your score will be tracked and displayed after each attempt.
  • Click on the 'Start Test' button when you are ready to begin.

Instructions for the WordGame Test

Welcome to the WordGame test. Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the test:

  • A word will be shown in the display area for 3 seconds before disappearing. This will be repeated for a total of 3 words.
  • After all the words have been displayed, enter the words you remember in the input field.
  • Click the "Voice Input" button to use speech recognition to enter the words.
  • Remember the exact order in which the words are shown. This is crucial for achieving a high accuracy score.
  • Click on the 'Start Test' button when you are ready to begin.

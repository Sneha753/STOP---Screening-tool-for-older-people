Instructions for the Flipcard Test

Welcome to the Flipcard test. Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the test:

  • This is a simple Flip card game to check Cognitive capacity.
  • Flip a card and match it with its pair.
  • Continue flipping cards and try to remember their positions to find matching pairs. Focus on remembering card positions and matching pairs.
  • Try to match all pairs in the fewest moves possible for a higher accuracy score.
  • Click on the 'Start Test' button when you are ready to begin.
